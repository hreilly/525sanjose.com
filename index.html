Modern Luxury in Historic Fig Garden

Just steps away from the shopping, dining, and culture of Fresno's Fig Garden Village, Granville's newest gated apartment community is full of the luxurious and energy-saving features our residents love the most. Featuring 1 and 2 bedroom floor plans, 525 San Jose's 42 unit complex includes attached garages, in-unit washers and dryers, security systems, and much more. In addition, the following policies will be in place until recommendations from health and governmental organizations change:

  • Outdoor spaces are still open, but we encourage you to practice the 6-foot social distancing rule recommended by the CDC.
  • All indoor shared common spaces are closed until further notice (gyms, clubhouses, etc.). Laundry rooms will remain open, but we encourage you to practice social distancing and sanitize surfaces before & after use.
  • In-person community events are cancelled until further notice.
  • We are encouraging everyone to make rent payments using our online payment portal. If you need to set up an online account, please contact your property manager.
  • If you are facing financial difficulties related to COVID-19, please reach out to your property manager as soon as possible. We're here to help.

We truly appreciate your patience and consideration as we all work together to protect our communities and our loved ones. Our whole team sincerely thanks you for helping us help each other.

Our office will still be open by appointment for new move-ins and for our valued vendors & partners. If you need to schedule an appointment, please contact the appropriate leasing office. Policies are subject to change at any time. We will be updating our operating procedures based on current recommendations from health and governmental organizations. Thank you for your patience and consideration during this time.
