Modern Luxury in Historic Fig Garden

Just steps away from the shopping, dining, and culture of Fresno's Fig Garden Village, Granville's newest gated apartment community is full of the luxurious and energy-saving features our residents love the most. Featuring 1 and 2 bedroom floor plans, 525 San Jose's 42 unit complex includes attached garages, in-unit washers and dryers, security systems, and much more.
